Welcome to SimBreviloquent

Tired of opening multiple windows to transcribe information from SimBrief into X-Plane?

Our new plugin simplifies your workflow by providing in-simulation pop-up windows with all the critical information you need. While you'll still enter the data manually, our streamlined pop-ups take up less screen real estate, making the process quicker and more efficient.

During initial flight preparation, when you're not in a critical stage of flight, use our floating menu that expands on hover to easily select the pop-up windows you need, such as:

  • Departure Airport
  • Arrival Airport
  • Flight Plan Summary
  • Load Sheet
  • Routing
  • ATC Flight Plan
  • Runway Analysis

For critical stages of flight, our plugin allows quick access to essential information, ensuring you have what you need at your fingertips without cluttering your screen. Pop out only the information you need, when you need it, and focus on flying, not on juggling windows!

Features

  • Seamless data download from SimBrief
  • Real-time updates in X-Plane
  • User-friendly interface

Installation

Follow these steps to install the SimBreviloquent plugin:

  1. Download the plugin from the GitHub repository.
  2. Extract the files into your X-Plane plugins folder.
  3. Launch X-Plane and configure the plugin settings.
